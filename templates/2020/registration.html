Registration

Before you fill out the expression of interest form at the bottom of this page, please read the following frequently asked questions carefully.

  • When does the school start and end?

    We start on Sat May 9th at 13:30 and end on Fri 15th at 12:00. Students should have arrived in Bertinoro before 13:00 on Sunday at the latest and are expected to stay the whole week.

  • How much does it cost?

    Full registration is â‚¬440. This covers accommodation for the whole week in shared rooms, breakfast, lunch as well as an excursion. Students will only have to pay for the cost of travel to Bertinoro and for their dinners in the many cosy, and reasonably priced, restaurants in the village.

  • Can I get support to attend the Summer School?

    If you would be unable to attend without financial assistance, then let us know: we have various funds available to support deserving students. We do require those to whom we offer assistance to be considerate and modest as our funds are not unlimited and different sources of funding come with different limitations. However, we will do our best to support you in attending PLISS. If you are accepted for PLISS, and have requested financial assistance, then please check with the organizers before booking any travel.

  • Who can attend?

    PLISS 2020 is aimed at mid/late stage PhD students and early postdocs working in and around programming languages. We will accept some applicants from other career stages, though PLISS 2021 will be aimed at more junior applicants. Note: previous attendees of PLISS are eligible to attend PLISS 2020.

  • Are there prerequisites?

    An undergraduate programming language class and/or compiler class is all you need.

The Expression of Interest Form

Once you have read the information above, please fill out PLISS 2020 expression of interest form by 2020-02-28. We will then notify applicants, successful or not, on or before 2020-03-13. Successful applicants will receive a link to the payment site which must be completed by 2020-03-25.
