PLISS: the next generation!

May 25-30, 2026

The Eighth International Programming Language Implementation Summer School (PLISS) will be held in Bertinoro in May 2026.

PLISS' goal is to prepare students for advanced study in the field through a combination of lectures on language implementation techniques, exploring the state of the art in language research and practice, and mentoring sessions.

PLISS is open to all.

Financial support is available for students in need.
