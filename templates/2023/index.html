2023-05-10: PLISS'23 announced

PLISS is back!

Sep 3-9, 2023

The Fifth International Programming Language Implementation Summer School (PLISS) will be held in Bertinoro in September 2023.

The Summer School's goal is to prepare students for advanced study in the field. This will be done through a combination of lectures on language implementation techniques, exploring the state of the art in programming language research and practice, and mentoring sessions.

PLISS is open to students at all levels.

Support for attendence is available for students who need it.

The co-located FoJ workshop will be open to students.
