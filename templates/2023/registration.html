Registration

Before you fill out the expression of interest form, read the following carefully.

  • When does PLISS start and end?

    We start on Mon Sept 4 at 8:30 and end on Sat 9th at 12:00. Students should have arrived in Bertinoro before the start and are expected to stay the whole week.

  • How much does it cost?

  • Can I get support to attend the Summer School?

    If you would be unable to attend without financial assistance, let us know. We have funds to support registrations of deserving students. We cannot cover travel costs though.

  • Who can attend?

    We welcome everyone interested in programming languages.

  • Are there prerequisites?

    An undergraduate programming language class and/or compiler class is all you need.
